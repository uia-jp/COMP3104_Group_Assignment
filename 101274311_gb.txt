----- About George Brown College -----

George Brown College (GBC) is a fully accredited post-secondary institution that provides students with the skills, industry experience and qualifications to pursue their career of choice. 
GBC offers over 300 programs across a variety of professions!

- Location: Toronto, Ontario, Canada
- Founded: 1967
- School Colors: White, Blue
- Campuses: Casa Loma campus, St. James campus, Waterfront campus
